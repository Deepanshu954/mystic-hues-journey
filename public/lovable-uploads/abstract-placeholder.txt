
The following abstract background images should be placed in this directory:

1. abstract-light.jpg - A high-quality light background for light mode
   Suggestion: A subtle, light-colored abstract pattern with soft warm tones
   
2. abstract-dark.jpg - A high-quality dark background for dark mode
   Suggestion: A deep, dark abstract pattern with subtle indigo/violet accents

These images will be used as the background for the application in light and dark modes.
